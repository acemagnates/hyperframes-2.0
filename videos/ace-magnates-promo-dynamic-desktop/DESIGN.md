# Design System

## Overview

Ace Magnates is a dark luxury membership brand for entrepreneurs and investors. The site uses a centered desktop layout, deep black surfaces, oxblood radial glows, red CTA accents, and a glass dashboard card as the hero proof object. Typography pairs high-contrast Playfair Display for prestige headlines with Inter for crisp UI, stats, and body copy. The experience feels premium, exclusive, and proof-driven rather than playful or casual.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#0A0A0A` - deep black page background and nav surface.
- **Panel Surface**: `#111111` - elevated cards and CTA box surfaces.
- **Primary Text**: `#FFFFFF` - hero headlines, stats, and core labels.
- **Muted Text**: `#B0B0B0` - body copy and secondary descriptions.
- **Brand Red**: `#C41E2E` - primary accent, gradients, CTAs, active nav states.
- **Bright Red**: `#E0333F` - badges, highlights, and energetic red glow.
- **Gold Accent**: `#D4A853` - premium feature accent and secondary highlight.
- **Success Green**: `#22C55E` - portfolio growth indicator.
- **Dark Red**: `#8B0000` - deep chart gradients and shadowed red elements.

## Typography

- **Display Serif**: Playfair Display (400, 700, 800, 900). Used for hero and section headlines; large, high-contrast, prestige-led.
- **Primary Sans**: Inter (300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900). Used for navigation, body, stats, cards, labels, and buttons.
- **Fallback**: Arial (400). Only for basic fallback rendering.
- **Scale**: Hero display around 80px; section headings around 51px; card headings 24px; body around 19px; labels at 16px or above for video legibility.

## Elevation

Depth comes from glassy dark panels with 1px translucent white borders, soft black shadows, and red radial glow rather than heavy drop shadows. Dashboard and feature cards use `#111111` panels, 20px radii, subtle borders, and restrained glow. CTAs use red gradients and a soft `rgba(196, 30, 46, 0.25)` bloom.

## Components

- **Prestige Navbar**: Logo lockup with white brand name, red sublabel, simple text links, and a red rounded CTA.
- **Open Membership Badge**: Pill label with red dot, red text, and a thin red border.
- **Hero Wealth Dashboard**: Glass panel showing portfolio amount, green growth pill, red bar chart, and three stat cards.
- **Feature Grid Cards**: Dark cards with luxury icons, white headings, and muted descriptions.
- **Proof Stats Strip**: Large red-gradient numeric stats for active members, revenue generated, businesses launched, and countries represented.
- **Testimonial Cards**: Dark glass cards with five-star social proof and circular initial avatars.
- **Final CTA Box**: Centered dark panel with "Ready to join the elite?" and paired action buttons.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use `#0A0A0A` and `#111111` as the dominant surfaces.
- Keep red as the active motion and conversion color.
- Use Playfair Display for the big emotional promise.
- Recreate the glass dashboard and stat cards as recognizable brand proof.
- Let the frame breathe with wide margins and cinematic negative space.

### Don'ts

- Do not introduce light backgrounds or bright generic SaaS colors.
- Do not replace the premium serif with a plain sans headline.
- Do not make the video text-only; the dashboard and stats must carry the story.
- Do not use busy decorative patterns that fight the luxury mood.
- Do not flatten everything into one shade of red; keep black, white, muted gray, green, and gold in the mix.
