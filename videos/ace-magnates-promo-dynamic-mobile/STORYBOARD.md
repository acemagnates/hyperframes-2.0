# Storyboard

**Format:** 1920x1080 landscape
**Audio:** Kokoro voiceover plus restrained premium underscore direction
**VO direction:** Confident, warm, focused; luxury membership promo with clean pauses.
**Style basis:** DESIGN.md, captured Ace Magnates hero screenshot, site source copy, and extracted tokens.

## Asset Audit

| Asset | Type | Assign to Beat | Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `capture/screenshots/scroll-000.png` | Captured hero screenshot | Beats 1 and 4 | Signature website visual, moving background/device panel |
| Recreated dashboard HTML | UI reconstruction | Beats 1 and 3 | Sharp animated portfolio proof object |
| Recreated feature cards | UI reconstruction | Beat 2 | Communicate mentorship, signals, academy, network |
| Recreated stats strip | UI reconstruction | Beat 3 | Animate real site statistics |
| Text logo lockup | HTML reconstruction | Beats 1 and 4 | Brand mark and final CTA |

## Beat 1 - Empire Hook (0.00-4.60s)

**VO:** "Build your empire with Ace Magnates."

**Concept:** The viewer enters a dark luxury command room. The captured website drifts behind the scene like a premium product reveal, while the brand lockup and hero promise assemble in front.

**Visual:** A full-frame black/oxblood environment with the captured hero screenshot tilted and softly glowing in the background. The Ace Magnates logo lockup lands top-left, the open membership badge draws in, and a large Playfair headline locks center-left. The glass portfolio dashboard floats on the right, with chart bars filling upward and the green gain pill pulsing once.

**Animation:** Screenshot slow zooms. Logo fades and rises. Badge slides in. Headline words cascade. Dashboard card floats in perspective. Chart bars fill staggered. Red glows drift slowly.

**Transition:** Blur-through into Beat 2.

## Beat 2 - What Members Get (4.60-9.80s)

**VO:** "It is the Millionaires Hub for ambitious entrepreneurs and investors building generational wealth. Get elite mentorship, investment signals, business academy playbooks, and a network that keeps you accountable."

**Concept:** The promo becomes a premium capability wall. The offer is concrete and useful: mentorship, signals, education, and network.

**Visual:** Four feature cards orbit into a disciplined grid over a dark surface: Elite Mentorship, Investment Signals, Business Academy, and Networking Events. Each card has a red/gold icon chip, compact body copy, and thin glass border. A red line draws across the top like a membership access scan.

**Animation:** Cards cascade upward, icon chips pop with glow, a line draw sweeps across, body copy rises, and subtle gold/red particles drift behind.

**Transition:** Whip pan into Beat 3.

## Beat 3 - Proof Stack (9.80-15.60s)

**VO:** "More than twelve thousand active members. Over fifty million dollars in member revenue generated. Five hundred businesses launched across forty seven countries."

**Concept:** Proof takes over the frame. Numbers are not just listed; they count up like a wealth dashboard coming online.

**Visual:** Four stat towers fill the frame with big red-gradient Playfair numbers: 12K+, $50M+, 500+, 47+. Behind them, the recreated portfolio dashboard expands and the bars climb in sync with the counters. Small labels keep everything legible and factual.

**Animation:** Counters count up from zero, bar chart fills, red halo blooms behind the most important stat, and the camera pushes forward slightly.

**Transition:** Cinematic zoom into Beat 4.

## Beat 4 - Join The Hub (15.60-20.00s)

**VO:** "Ace Magnates. Join the Hub."

**Concept:** Final brand close. The noise clears, the logo becomes the hero, and the CTA feels exclusive and immediate.

**Visual:** The captured hero screenshot resolves into a tilted background panel. Front and center: Ace Magnates Millionaires Hub, "Ready to join the elite?", and a red Join Now button. Supporting proof chips sit below: members, revenue, satisfaction.

**Animation:** Background screenshot eases into focus, brand title scales in, CTA button glows and breathes, proof chips slide in, final red underline draws under the brand name.

**Transition:** Final fade to black in the last half-second.
